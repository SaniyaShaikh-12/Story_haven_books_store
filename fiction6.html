Author : Sofiya PasterNack

Highlights :

  • Plot Overview: Anya and the Nightingale is a captivating fantasy adventure set in medieval Europe. The story follows Anya, a Jewish girl with magical powers, as she embarks on a dangerous quest to rescue her friend Ivan, who has been kidnapped by a mythical firebird
