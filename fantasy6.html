The Hurricane Wars

355/- 455/-

The Hurricane Wars follows the story of Kael, a young warrior caught in the midst of a fierce conflict between rival kingdoms. As hurricanes ravage the land, Kael discovers that the storms are not just natural disasters but manifestations of ancient magic tied to the struggles between the kingdoms. When a powerful artifact that could end the wars is uncovered, Kael must choose between loyalty to his people and the greater good. Alongside a group of misfit allies, he embarks on a perilous quest filled with treachery, unexpected alliances, and the fight for survival against the elements and their enemies.

Author : Thea Guanzon
