Nobody Knows But You

255/-355/-

Nobody Knows But You centers on Kayla, who dies under mysterious circumstances at a summer camp. The story is told through letters, texts, and emails from her best friend, Lainie, as she tries to unravel the truth about what happened. As Lainie reflects on their friendship, jealousy, betrayal, and the darker side of camp life come into focus, making her question everything she thought she knew about Kayla and those around her. The novel weaves together suspense and emotional complexity, exploring the thin line between friendship and obsession.

Author : Anica Mrose Rissi
