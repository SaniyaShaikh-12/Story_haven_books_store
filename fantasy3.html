The Queen Of Nothing

399/- 499/-

Queen of Nothing by Holly Black is a captivating fantasy novel that concludes the Folk of the Air trilogy. It follows Jude Duarte as she navigates the treacherous world of Faerie, where she must confront her desires, alliances, and the consequences of power.

Author : Holly Black

Highlights :

  • Plot Overview: In Queen of Nothing, Jude Duarte is exiled from the Faerie realm after the betrayal of her beloved Cardan. Determined to reclaim her place and power, Jude must navigate political intrigue and personal challenges. As she grapples with her feelings for Cardan and her thirst for vengeance, Jude’s journey takes her through dangerous alliances and heart-wrenching decisions that will determine the fate of the Faerie realm.
  • Themes : he story explores the complexities of power dynamics and the lengths one will go to achieve their goals. Relationships are tested, highlighting themes of loyalty and the pain of betrayal. Jude’s struggles reflect her quest for acceptance in a world that constantly challenges her..
  • Genre: Fantasy, Identity and Belonging, Power and Ambition.
  • Pages: 400 pages
  • Publication Year: 2021

Why You’ll Love It

  • Jude is a fiercely determined character whose growth and resilience resonate with readers.
  • The Faerie realm is vividly crafted, full of intricate lore and magical elements that draw you in..
  • The relationship between Jude and Cardan is fraught with tension, passion, and complexity.
  • The story is filled with unexpected twists and turns, keeping you hooked until the very last page.
