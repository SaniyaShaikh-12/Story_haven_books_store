The King Of Wrath

$18.99 $20.99

King of Wrath is a contemporary romance that centers on Wrath, a powerful billionaire known for his ruthlessness, and Célia, a spirited woman who challenges his guarded nature. As they enter a high-stakes arrangement, their fiery chemistry ignites a passionate romance filled with emotional turmoil. Facing external threats and personal demons, they must navigate love and trust while uncovering whether they can rely on each other amidst the chaos of their intertwined lives.

Author : Ana Huang

Highlights :

  • Plot Overview: King of Wrath follows the story of Wrath, a powerful and enigmatic billionaire who is known for his ruthless demeanor in the business world. When he becomes embroiled in a high-stakes arrangement with a spirited woman named Célia, sparks fly. As their lives intertwine, Célia challenges Wrath’s guarded nature, leading to a passionate yet tumultuous romance. However, their relationship faces external threats from rival factions and personal demons that force them to confront their deepest fears.
  • Themes : The book explores themes of power, trust, and redemption. It delves into the complexities of love in the face of ambition and the struggle to break down emotional barriers to achieve genuine connection.
  • Genre: fiction, Contemporary Romance, New Adult.
  • Pages: 456 pages
  • Publication Year: 2020

Why You’ll Love It

  • Both Wrath and Célia have compelling character arcs, making their emotional journeys engaging and relatable.
  • Steamy Romance: The chemistry between the leads is intense and passionate, filled with thrilling romantic moments.
  • High-Stakes Drama: The plot includes elements of suspense and intrigue, keeping readers on the edge of their seats.
  • Emotional Depth: The exploration of vulnerability and the impact of past trauma adds layers of emotional complexity to the story.
