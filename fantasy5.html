To Dream Of White and Gold

445/- 559/-

To Dream of White and Gold follows the journey of a young woman named Elara, who lives in a world where magic is tied to emotions and the color of one's heart. When her family is threatened by a dark force seeking to harness this magic, Elara discovers her own unique abilities. Alongside a charming stranger with secrets of his own, she embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind her family's legacy and protect her realm. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and face their deepest fears, Elara learns about love, sacrifice, and the true meaning of strength.
