The Education Challenge is a non-fiction book that explores the complexities and issues facing the global education system. It addresses challenges such as inequality in access, outdated teaching methods, lack of resources, and the impact of technology on learning. The book provides insights from educators, policymakers, and students, offering solutions to improve the quality and accessibility of education worldwide. Through a blend of data and personal stories, it highlights the need for reform and innovation to equip future generations with the skills they need for an evolving world.

Author : Stacy Willingham
