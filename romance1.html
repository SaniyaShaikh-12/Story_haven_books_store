Twisted Love

$18.99 $20.99

T Twisted Love is a contemporary romance that explores the passionate and complicated relationship between Alex Volkov, a photographer haunted by his past, and Eva Chang, his best friend’s younger sister. When Eva moves in with Alex, their undeniable chemistry leads to a tumultuous romance filled with emotional challenges and family expectations. As they navigate their feelings and confront their scars, they must decide if their love can survive the complexities of their intertwined lives.

Author : Ana Huang

Highlights :

  • Plot Overview: Twisted Love follows the intense relationship between Alex Volkov, a successful photographer with a dark past, and Eva Chang, his best friend's younger sister. When Eva moves in with Alex, their chemistry ignites, leading to a passionate romance. However, they must navigate emotional scars, family expectations, and the risks of loving someone who could change their lives forever. As their bond deepens, they question whether their love can withstand the complexities of their intertwined lives.
  • Themes : The book explores themes of love, friendship, and healing from trauma. It delves into the complexities of relationships, the struggle for personal identity, and the power of vulnerability in building deep connections.
  • Genre: Contemporary Romance, New Adult.
  • Pages: 496 pages
  • Publication Year: 2022

Why You’ll Love It

  • Dual Perspectives: The alternating viewpoints provide insight into each character’s thoughts and feelings, enhancing the reader's connection to their story.
