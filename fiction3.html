Frankenstein

433/- 566/-

Frankenstein tells the story of Victor Frankenstein, a young scientist who creates a living being by reanimating a dead body through a scientific experiment. Horrified by the creature's appearance, Victor abandons it. The creature, shunned by society, becomes vengeful and seeks revenge on Victor, leading to a tragic series of events. Shelley's novel explores themes of ambition, the consequences of playing God, and the search for identity and belonging.

Author : Mary Shelly
