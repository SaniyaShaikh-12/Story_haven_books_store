It Starts With Us

$18.99 $20.99

It Starts With Us follows Lily Bloom as she navigates the complexities of love and co-parenting after establishing a new life in Boston. Struggling with her feelings for her estranged husband, Ryle, and her new partner, Atlas, Lily learns to prioritize her well-being and create a safe environment for her daughter. The story explores themes of love, forgiveness, and personal growth, emphasizing the importance of making choices that lead to happiness and healing.

Author : Colleen Hoover

Highlights :

  • Plot Overview:It Starts With Us continues the story of Lily Bloom and her journey of love, healing, and self-discovery. After establishing her life in Boston and finding happiness with a new partner, Lily is faced with the challenges of co-parenting her daughter with her estranged husband, Ryle. The narrative explores the complexities of love, as Lily navigates her feelings for both Ryle and her new love interest, Atlas. Through personal struggles, Lily learns about the importance of boundaries, self-worth, and the transformative power of love as she strives to create a safe and loving environment for her child.
  • Themes : The book delves into themes of love, forgiveness, and the impact of past relationships on personal growth. It emphasizes the significance of making choices that prioritize well-being and happiness, as well as the strength it takes to break free from toxic patterns..
  • Genre: Contemporary Romance
  • Pages: 320 pages
  • Publication Year: 2022

Why You’ll Love It

  • Lily’s journey is relatable and showcases her growth as she confronts her past and embraces her future.
  • The story tackles complex emotions and relationships, resonating with readers who appreciate heartfelt narratives.
  • The exploration of love, parenting, and healing reflects real-life challenges, making it a thought-provoking read.
  • Fans of Hoover will enjoy her engaging writing style and ability to weave emotional stories that stay with you long after reading.
